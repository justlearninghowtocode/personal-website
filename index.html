Things That Inspire Me

From my passion for urban planning, I believe in the importance of connecting places and people. From the ongoing Pandamic we all have become increasingly aware of how important it is to stay connected to the people around us.

RIPAS Bridge

I have used the RIPAS bridge a number of times back and from work and I love the view of Bandar Seri Begawan and the Water Village while driving. I believe that the bridge will help to spur development across the Brunei River and help with affordability in the capital.

Urban Planning Video

I believe in the power of good urban planning to improve the lives of people. A well designed city is not just good for the environment and the economy, but also for the spritual and emotional welbeing of the city's residents. I believe through the use of computing technology, we will be better able to plan and design our cities to meet the needs of the 21st Century.

SHOAS Bridge

This bridge has played a vital part in keeping country connected during the ongoing COVID-19 pandamic. The bridge is a modern marvel of engineering which not only serves its primary role in linking Temburong with the rest of Brunei but also to protect the environment and for the traffic of boats from Bandar Seri Begawan to the wider world ocean.
